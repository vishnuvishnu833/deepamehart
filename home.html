Frequently Asked Questions

Is the advance amount refundable?
The advance amount secures your booking date and time slot, and is therefore non-refundable. This allows us to dedicate our time and resources exclusively to your event.
When should I book my mehendi appointment?
We recommend booking at least 3-4 weeks in advance, especially during peak wedding seasons (October-February). For popular dates, we advise booking 2-3 months ahead to ensure availability.
Do you provide henna services outside Lucknow?
Yes, we proudly serve clients throughout Uttar Pradesh and beyond. Destination services include travel and accommodation charges, which we customize based on your location and event requirements.
Are the henna colours natural and safe?
Absolutely! We use only 100% natural, premium quality henna that is safe for all skin types. Our special blend creates a rich, dark stain without any harmful chemicals or additives.
How long does the mehendi application take?
Bridal mehendi typically takes 4-6 hours depending on the complexity of design and coverage. Guest mehendi takes about 30-75 minutes per person based on design choice.
How can I make my mehendi last longer?
For best results, keep the henna paste on for at least 6-8 hours. After removal, avoid water contact for 12 hours. Apply a mixture of lemon juice and sugar to darken the stain. Moisturize regularly with natural oils to maintain the color.
