Frequently Asked Questions

Is the advance amount refundable?
The advance amount secures your booking date and time slot in our schedule, and is therefore non-refundable. However, in case of exceptional circumstances, we may offer to reschedule your appointment based on availability.
When should I book my mehendi appointment?
We recommend booking at least 3-4 weeks in advance, especially during peak wedding seasons (November-February). For popular dates and weekends, booking 2-3 months ahead is advisable to ensure availability. Last-minute bookings are subject to availability with a premium charge.
Do you provide services outside Lucknow?
Yes, we provide henna services throughout Uttar Pradesh and for destination weddings across India. Travel charges vary based on distance and may include accommodation if required. We've served clients in Delhi NCR, Jaipur, Varanasi, and other cities with prior arrangement.
Are the henna colors natural and safe?
Absolutely! We use only 100% natural, chemical-free henna imported directly from Rajasthan. Our special blend includes essential oils and natural ingredients that ensure a rich, dark stain without any skin irritation. We never use black henna or any products containing PPD.
How long does the application take?
Bridal mehendi typically takes 3-6 hours depending on the complexity of design and coverage (hands, arms, feet). Guest mehendi takes about 20-45 minutes per person depending on the design chosen. We recommend allocating extra time for the paste to dry before removal.
How long will the mehendi last?
With proper aftercare, our henna designs typically last 1-3 weeks. The color develops over 24-48 hours, reaching its richest shade. Duration varies based on skin type, aftercare, and area of application (hands usually last longer than feet).

About The Artist

Deepa, the creative force behind our studio, is a passionate mehendi artist with over 12 years of experience specializing in bridal and festive henna. Trained under master artists in Jaipur and Dubai, she brings a unique fusion of traditional Indian and contemporary Arabic styles to her work.

What began as a childhood fascination with the art form blossomed into a lifelong dedication after she designed henna for her sister's wedding at just 16. Since then, Deepa has adorned over 500 brides with her intricate designs, each piece telling its own beautiful story.

Her philosophy centers on creating not just beautiful designs, but meaningful connections with each client. "Every bride deserves to feel like royalty on her special day," Deepa says. "My art is about enhancing that radiance and creating memories that linger long after the celebrations."

- Deepa
